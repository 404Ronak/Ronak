About TripTailor

🧑‍💻 Who are we?

We are a small team of travel lovers and developers based in Gujarat, India. This website was created and maintained by Ronak Patel, a passionate builder who believes smart technology can simplify planning and make travel stress-free.

❓ Why did we build this?

We noticed how frustrating it can be to plan a trip — searching blogs, comparing options, and figuring out what fits your time and budget. That’s why we built TripTailor — to generate complete travel plans in seconds.

🎯 What is our mission?

Our mission is simple: Make travel planning effortless, personalized, and accessible to everyone. We want every traveler to enjoy a well-organized trip without spending hours preparing for it.

💡 What makes TripTailor different?

  • ✅ Instant 7-day customized plans based on your input
  • 📥 Downloadable offline PDFs
  • 💰 Local to luxury budget filters
  • 📍 Gujarat-focused recommendations (for now, more coming soon!)

🚀 What's next?

We’re working on adding AI-generated suggestions, day-wise editable plans, and user-submitted itineraries. Stay tuned!
